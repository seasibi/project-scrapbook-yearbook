SCRAPBOOK DECORATION ITEMS
==========================

Drop your Canva cut-out decoration PNGs here. They will appear scattered
around the gallery as scrapbook embellishments.

Naming — match the slot labels exactly:

  scrap1.png   scrap2.png   scrap3.png   (gallery section 1)
  scrap4.png   scrap5.png   scrap6.png   (gallery section 2)
  scrap7.png   scrap8.png               (gallery section 3)

Supported formats: .png  .jpg  .jpeg  .webp  .avif
PNG with transparency works best so the paper background shows through.

Any slot without a matching file is simply invisible — no placeholder shown.
